<b>Near The Fear is a roguelite racing game</b> with a theme of dealing with fear, set in surreal world of subconscious.<br/>
 * Drive on procedurally generated tracks that change based on your playstyle<br/>
 * Use special abilities to either face the danger or avoid it<br/>
 * Get random upgrades and change how you play each run <br/>
