Two spaceships on the run! You can only take <b>45° turns</b>,
scrolling at lightspeed in the winding corridors of a gigantic spaceship.
Will you take the risk of going faster, or will you let your opponent outrun you?
<br/>
<br/>
You earn points each time your opponent goes off the camera’s range.
After 3 minutes, the player who has the most points wins!
<br/>
<br/>
Want to know if you are really good at the game? <b>Try to beat the AI!</b>
